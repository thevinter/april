Flight Category Reference

VFR Visual Flight Rules — Ceiling >900 m, Visibility >8 km. Clear skies, fly freely.
MVFR Marginal VFR — Ceiling 300–900 m, Visibility 5–8 km. Caution advised. Consult your wind rider master.
IFR Instrument Flight Rules — Ceiling 60–300 m, Visibility 1.6–5 km. Instrument rating required. Or a very brave gryphon.
LIFR Low IFR — Ceiling <60 m, Visibility <1.6 km. Do not fly. Seriously. Walk. Hearthstone. Anything else.
