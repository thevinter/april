Key Depletion Coverage

Our flagship product. Key Depletion Coverage provides financial protection when your Mythic+ keystone is depleted due to circumstances beyond your reasonable control.

What's Covered

  • Depletion due to group member disconnection (verified via combat logs)
  • Depletion due to inadequate DPS from PUG members (Details! screenshot required)
  • Depletion due to failure to meet timer by less than 30 seconds ("heartbreaker" clause)
  • Depletion caused by incorrect routing by a PUG leader (MDT evidence accepted)
  • Depletion from wipes on trash due to missed interrupts (interrupt tracker required)

Ninja Looting Protection

Ninja Looting Protection provides compensation when loot is awarded to a group member who should not have received it under standard PUG etiquette guidelines.

Covered Scenarios

  • Need roll on off-spec gear without group consent
  • Master Looter abuse (legacy raids/timewalking)
  • Personal Loot trading refusal on clearly-not-an-upgrade items
  • Vault reward disputes in coordinated PUG raids
  • "Accidentally" selecting Need on BoE items valued over 50,000g

Compensation

Claims are valued at the item's current Auction House market value, or the item's estimated DPS/HPS upgrade value, whichever is greater. Compensation is paid in gold-equivalent credit toward your next premium.

Important: Ninja Looting Protection does not cover items that the ninja looter could legitimately use. Our adjusters will verify spec compatibility through the Armory API.

Tank Rage-Quit Rider

An optional rider available with Gold and Mythic plans. Covers the unique damages incurred when a tank abandons the group mid-dungeon.

What Triggers Coverage

  • Tank leaves after a single wipe ("one-and-done" clause)
  • Tank leaves after being asked to slow down
  • Tank leaves because "DPS is too low" (before first boss)
  • Tank disconnects and never returns (presumed rage-quit after 5 minutes)
  • Tank switches to DPS spec mid-dungeon without notice

Payout Structure

Compensation includes: key depletion reimbursement + queue time damages (calculated at $0.50 per minute of estimated tank queue replacement time) + emotional distress flat fee of $5.00.
